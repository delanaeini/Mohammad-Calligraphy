About me


I started Calligraphy at 14 years old, learning Tahriri Nastaliq with Master Vahid Alipour. As my interest in calligraphy grew, I started self studying Nastaliq using reeds and copying the works and method of Grand Master Amirkhani, and I continued working on Nastaliq and Shekasteh for four more years. In 2020, I started learning Nastaliq with Master Yadegar Khayyam, while continuing to work on Shekasteh.

I recevied the Distingushed (Mumtaz) Certificate from The Calligraphers Association of Iran on Nastaliq in 2022, and I reveived the Distingushed (Mumtaz) Certificate on Shekasteh in 2023.
